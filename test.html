Bio

I currently live in a lake house in Coventry, CT with my little dog, Douglas, who is the love of my life. This summer I started working as a software engineer for Plex, a totally remote company that makes media server software. I currently work on the smart speakers team, so I write middleware that allows you to stream music from your server to all of your smart speakers. I get to listen to music all day. I'm a long distance runner and belong to a makerspace where I do some woodworking and play with lasers.

Experience

  • I also participated in the Product Development Project in Helsinki, Finland, working with engineering and design students to develop products for Finnish companies.
  • I became involved with FIRST robotics, volunteering at many FTC tournaments where high schoolers build amazing robots that play games. I also coached a Lego robotics team on the Upper East Side. I used to teach the Lego robotics class at Pace until I moved too far away from NYC.

  • While teaching, I completed my MS in Computer Science here at Pace - I made a smart mirror for my thesis project. I finished my MS in Spring 2018.
  • Now I'm a software engineer for Plex!
